Pattern Detected

Property crime clusters in southeast corridor and downtown Greeley during evening hours (6-11 PM). Motor vehicle theft shows strongest geographic concentration — 68% within 3 zones.

Forecast

With population growing at 2.1% annually and calls for service rising 5% per year, current staffing levels will reach capacity by Q3 2027 without predictive deployment optimization.

Anomaly

Arson clearance rate improved 135% — significantly outpacing other categories. Suggests investigative process change or resource reallocation that could be replicated for burglary (lowest clearance improvement).

Recommendation

Deploy predictive patrol routing using RTIC camera feeds + historical incident data. Model estimates 12-18% additional reduction in property crime through optimized officer placement during peak hours.
