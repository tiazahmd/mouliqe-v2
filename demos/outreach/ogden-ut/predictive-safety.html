AI-Generated Insights

Lefgren's Vision Realized

Connecting Waze traffic data with OnStar vehicle data and ATAC dispatch could reduce average emergency response time by 30-90 seconds — the difference Andy described in the Pure Storage case study. With 8 years of ATAC historical data, the ML model can predict incident likelihood by zone, time, and conditions.

Camera Network Optimization

The Axis camera network drove a 20% crime reduction (KSL, 2019). Flock Safety LPR data adds vehicle tracking. Combining both with predictive hotspot mapping could optimize camera placement and patrol routing — targeting the remaining crime clusters that static camera positions miss.

Diminishing Returns Warning

After 55% reduction, further gains from traditional policing alone will plateau. The next 15% requires predictive deployment — putting officers where incidents are about to happen, not where they already happened. This is where ML on your Pure Storage infrastructure becomes essential.

Recommendation

Build a 3-layer analytics stack on Pure Storage: (1) Historical trend engine using 8 years of ATAC data, (2) Real-time enrichment layer connecting Waze + Flock + cameras, (3) Predictive deployment model generating shift-by-shift patrol recommendations. Estimated impact: additional 12-18% crime reduction over 24 months.
