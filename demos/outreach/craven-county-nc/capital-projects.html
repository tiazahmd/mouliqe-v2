What This Dashboard Demonstrates

Assistant County Manager Gene Hodges oversees $30M+ in active capital projects — from water system expansions to facility renovations to emergency infrastructure. Today, project status lives in contractor reports, spreadsheets, and meeting updates. This concept dashboard shows how a unified project portfolio tracker can give the County Manager's office real-time visibility into budget execution, timeline risk, and funding source compliance across all 18 active projects — replacing the manual status-gathering process with automated, data-driven oversight.
