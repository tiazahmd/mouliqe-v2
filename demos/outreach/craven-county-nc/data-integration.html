What This Dashboard Demonstrates

County Manager Jack Veit led Craven County's first ERP implementation in 20 years — proving the county's appetite for technology modernization. But the ERP only covers finance. The other 7+ systems (IBM mainframe, ArcGIS, Tyler Odyssey, Sheriff RMS, Water SCADA, CivicPlus) remain disconnected. With a 9-person IT team managing 31 departments, there's no bandwidth to build the integration layer internally. This concept dashboard shows what becomes possible when those systems are connected: real-time executive visibility, cross-department analytics, and the data-driven governance that a $161M budget demands.
