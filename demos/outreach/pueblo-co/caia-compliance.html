CAIA Requirements

What Deployers Must Do by June 30

1. AI Impact Assessment

Document each high-risk AI system: purpose, data inputs, outputs, potential for algorithmic discrimination, mitigation measures.

2. Governance Framework

Establish policies for AI system oversight, human review processes, and ongoing monitoring. Assign accountability.

3. Public Notice

Publish notice that AI systems are in use, what decisions they inform, and how residents can contest AI-influenced decisions.

4. Ongoing Monitoring

Continuous monitoring for bias, accuracy degradation, and unintended consequences. Annual reassessment required.
