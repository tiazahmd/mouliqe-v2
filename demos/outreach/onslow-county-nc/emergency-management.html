What This Dashboard Demonstrates

Onslow County's Emergency Services division manages EMS, Fire Rescue, Emergency Management, and E-911 — but each operates on separate systems with no unified operational picture. For a coastal county adjacent to Camp Lejeune with hurricane season approaching, real-time cross-system visibility isn't a luxury — it's a necessity. This concept dashboard shows how integrating CAD, EMS records, fire incident data, and shelter management into a single analytics layer gives Deputy Director Stacie Miles the situational awareness needed for both daily operations and disaster response.
