OLDCC Study Scope

The $1.6M OLDCC grant funds engineering analysis of infrastructure dependencies between Onslow County civilian systems and Camp Lejeune military installations. Tetra Tech handles the physical engineering assessment. The data analytics layer — mapping dependencies, modeling cascade failures, and building the decision-support dashboard — is the complementary capability Mouliqe provides.

What This Dashboard Demonstrates

Onslow County's $1.6M OLDCC infrastructure resiliency study generates complex dependency data across 47 critical systems shared between civilian and military installations. Tetra Tech provides the engineering analysis — but the data analytics layer that maps dependencies, models cascade failures, and powers the decision-support dashboard is a separate capability. This concept shows how Mouliqe can complement the engineering study with the analytics infrastructure that turns raw dependency data into actionable resiliency intelligence for ITS Director Arek Kempinski and Emergency Management.
