Resolution 3405 — Development Review Process Improvement

The Problem

Apartment demand is surging (WESH reporting). Multi-family applications up 83%. Current review process can't keep pace — 18 days average vs. 10-day target. Developers are waiting.

What Central Square Provides

Central Square handles permit intake and tracking. But it doesn't provide analytics — processing time trends, bottleneck identification, workload forecasting, or fee revenue projections.

What Analytics Adds

Identify which review stages cause delays, forecast permit volume by type, optimize staff allocation, project fee revenue, and give Development Services the data to meet the 10-day target.

Sanford approved Resolution 3405 to improve development review. Analytics is how you measure the improvement.

Central Square captures the data. Mouliqe turns it into insights that help Development Services hit their targets.
