What This Dashboard Demonstrates

Gaston County's newly established Grants Oversight & Development Division manages 39 active grants totaling $62.4M — including $40M in opioid settlement funds over 18 years. With the division page still showing "Coming soon," there's a greenfield opportunity to build the analytics infrastructure from day one. This concept dashboard shows how a unified grant management platform can give Pat Laws and the Grants Office real-time visibility into fund utilization, compliance deadlines, and outcome metrics across all 8 departments — replacing the spreadsheets and manual tracking that typically accompany a new grants office.
