What This Dashboard Demonstrates

Janet Schafer's Process Improvement Division uses Lean Six Sigma to drive efficiency across Gaston County's 26 departments — but measuring the impact of those improvements requires data that currently lives in 15+ separate vendor platforms. This concept dashboard shows how a unified analytics layer can track DMAIC project pipelines, measure cycle time reductions, quantify cost savings, and give the County Manager's office real-time visibility into the ROI of process improvement initiatives. The data becomes the evidence that justifies continued investment in operational excellence.
