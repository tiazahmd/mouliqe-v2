What This Dashboard Demonstrates

Gaston County's $40M opioid settlement is the largest single funding stream the county will manage over the next 15 years. The NC MOA requires annual reporting to the NC DOJ on approved-use compliance, community impact metrics, and fund utilization. Today, this tracking likely happens in spreadsheets across HHS, the Sheriff's Office, and GEMS. This concept dashboard shows how a purpose-built analytics layer can automate compliance reporting, track outcome metrics across agencies, and give the Board of Commissioners real-time visibility into how settlement funds are translating into community impact.
