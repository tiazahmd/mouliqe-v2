Program Effectiveness

Violence Reduction Programs — Outcome Tracking

Group Violence Intervention (GVI)

Targeted outreach to highest-risk individuals. Custom notifications, social service connections, community accountability.

Impact: Primary driver of 80% gun violence reduction

VCRI (Safe Racine)

Violence Crime Reduction Initiative — multi-agency partnership. Hospital-based intervention, street outreach, case management.

Impact: Repeat victimization down significantly

Community Camera Program

Residents register private cameras with PD via Polimorphic. Voluntary network creates coverage map for investigations.

Impact: Faster case resolution, community trust

Gun Lock Distribution

Free gun locks via public health vending machines and community events. Harm reduction approach to firearm safety.

Impact: Accidental discharge prevention
