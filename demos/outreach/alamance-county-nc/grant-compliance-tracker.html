What This Dashboard Demonstrates

Alamance County manages $38.8M+ in active federal and state grants across 8 departments — each with different reporting portals, compliance requirements, and deadlines. Today, this data lives in spreadsheets, email threads, and individual department files. This concept dashboard shows how a unified grant management layer can provide real-time visibility into fund utilization, flag compliance risks before they become audit findings, and generate the cross-program analytics needed for 2 CFR Part 200 reporting.
