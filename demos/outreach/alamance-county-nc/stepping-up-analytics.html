What This Dashboard Demonstrates

Alamance County's Stepping Up Initiative generates valuable cross-agency data across the Sheriff's Office, Detention Center, Vaya Health, Social Services, and the Family Justice Center — but today that data lives in separate systems with no unified view. This concept dashboard shows what becomes possible when those data streams are integrated: real-time risk stratification, outcome tracking, cost-per-diversion analysis, and the grant-ready metrics needed for BJA reporting and Stepping Up Innovation County recognition.
