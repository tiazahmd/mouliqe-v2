What This Dashboard Demonstrates

Alamance County's 2027 reappraisal will affect 78,500+ parcels across 15 communities. Tax Administrator Brad Fowler's team currently uses the 2023 Schedule of Values and Spatial Est for property records — but there's no predictive modeling tool to simulate revenue-neutral scenarios, identify equity impacts across income tiers, or forecast appeal volumes before the revaluation hits. This concept dashboard shows how analytics can transform the reappraisal from a reactive process into a proactive, data-driven planning exercise — reducing citizen complaints, optimizing the revenue-neutral rate, and giving Commissioners the data they need to make informed decisions.
