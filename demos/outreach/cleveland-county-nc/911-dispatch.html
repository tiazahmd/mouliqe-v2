What This Dashboard Demonstrates

Cleveland County is building a new 911 communications center as part of the Justice Center campus — expanding from 8 to 12 dispatch positions. This is the perfect moment to build analytics into the foundation: call volume forecasting for shift optimization, response time tracking by zone, call type analysis for resource allocation, and performance dashboards for Captain Hutchins and County Manager Cotton. Building this before the center opens means the analytics are ready on day one — not retrofitted after workflows are established.
