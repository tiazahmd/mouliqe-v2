What This Dashboard Demonstrates

Cleveland County Sheriff Alan Norman's office operates 12 ALPR cameras generating 2.4M+ plate reads annually, plus a $483K BJA grant specifically designed to "test a data-driven hypothesis" for traffic and pedestrian safety. The data exists — but the analytics don't. This concept dashboard shows how integrating ALPR data with incident reports, patrol zone analytics, and crime trends creates the intelligence layer that turns raw data into evidence-based policing decisions and the grant-compliant outcome metrics BJA requires.
