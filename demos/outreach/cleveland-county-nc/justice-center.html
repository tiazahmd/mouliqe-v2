What This Dashboard Demonstrates

Cleveland County is investing $100M+ in a new Justice Center campus that will physically consolidate the Sheriff's Office, Courts, Detention Center, and 911/EOC under one roof. But without a data integration strategy, these agencies will continue operating on separate systems — just in a nicer building. This concept dashboard shows what a "smart campus" looks like: unified data flows from arrest to disposition, real-time detention analytics, 911-to-outcome tracking, and ALPR intelligence. The window to build this right is now — before the campus opens and legacy workflows calcify.
