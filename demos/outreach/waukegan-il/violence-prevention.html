What Racine Did

Comparable City — 80% Gun Violence Reduction

Racine, WI (pop. 77K) — Before

42 people struck by gunfire. 13 homicides. Multiple violence reduction programs running with no data infrastructure to measure outcomes.

Racine, WI — After (with analytics)

9 people struck by gunfire (-79%). 4 homicides (-69%). GVI, VCRI, Community Camera Program — all tracked with outcome dashboards. Data proved what worked, defunded what didn't.

What Waukegan Could Do

Same approach: connect BJA grant programs to outcome data. Track intervention effectiveness. Show funders measurable results. Secure continued funding.
