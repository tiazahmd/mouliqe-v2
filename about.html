From Marketing to Data

A decade of turning campaign data into business decisions.

Having spent significant time on both sides of that divide, I bring the ability to bridge that gap - understanding the business context deeply enough to define the right problem, and the technical depth to architect the right solution.

That foundation is what eventually pulled me deeper into analytics. And AI - that's something I've been working with long before it became a buzzword. I built my first AI application years ago, back when the tools were rough and the results were honestly not great. But it gave me a head start that compounded over time.
