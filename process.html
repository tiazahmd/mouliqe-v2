Process

The Mouliqe Way

Six stages from understanding your problem to supporting the solution long after it's built. Transparent about scope, timeline, and expectations at every step.

00

Discovery

A conversation where I learn about your business, your challenges, and what you're trying to achieve. If I can't genuinely help, I'll tell you that too.

What you get

>Honest assessment of your situation

>Initial direction on the right approach

>Clear next steps if we move forward

What you do

>30 minutes on Google Meet

>No prep needed — just show up

>Tell me what's on your mind

01

Diagnosis

An in-depth evaluation of what you actually need — whether that's AI, automation, a data overhaul, or something else. Think of this as the proposal. You'll know what I'm recommending and why.

What you get

>Clear recommendation on the right approach

>Scope, timeline, and cost estimate

>Enough to make an informed decision

What you do

>Share relevant context and data access

>Identify key stakeholders

>Decide whether to move forward

02

Blueprint

The detailed technical architecture — platforms, data flows, integrations, and how everything connects and scales. The Blueprint is a deliverable in itself. It's the plan everything gets built from.

What you get

>Technical architecture document

>Platform and tooling recommendations

>Data flow diagrams and integration plan

>Phased implementation roadmap

What you do

>Provide system access and documentation

>Review and approve the architecture

>Sign off on the implementation plan

03

Build

Where the solution comes to life. Implementation is phased and milestone-driven — you see progress at every step, not just at the end. Each milestone is a checkpoint.

What you get

>Working system, delivered in phases

>Milestone demos and progress updates

>Documentation and handoff materials

What you do

>Review milestone deliverables

>Provide feedback and domain expertise

>Test with real data and scenarios

04

Refine

No system is perfect on day one. After the initial build, I tune, validate, and iterate until the solution performs the way it should in your real environment — not just in theory.

What you get

>Performance tuning and optimization

>Edge case handling and fixes

>Validation against real-world data

What you do

>Run the system with your team

>Flag issues and edge cases

>Confirm it meets expectations

05

Support

Every engagement includes two months of post-project support. I'm available to address issues, answer questions, and make adjustments as your team settles in. Need more? We can set that up separately.

What you get

>Two months of included support

>Bug fixes and adjustments

>Knowledge transfer to your team

What you do

>Use the system in production

>Report any issues or questions

>Onboard your team
