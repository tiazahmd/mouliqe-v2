Supply Chain Intelligence Engine

AI + Statistical

A hybrid intelligence engine that ingests commodity prices, shipping indices, currency rates, inflation indicators, internal inventory and procurement data, and global news — then uses statistical models for the math and AI agents for the reasoning to produce actionable recommendations.

How It Works

This engine processes 12 live signals across three domains simultaneously:

Market data — commodity prices, shipping indices, currency rates, inflation indicators (CPI, PPI)

Company data — inventory levels, procurement costs, supplier contracts, sales velocity

News & policy — tariffs, geopolitical events, disruptions, regulatory changes

Statistical models detect trends, correlations, and anomalies. AI agents classify news, reason about causality, and synthesize recommendations. An optimization model calculates the numbers. The result: specific, actionable decisions across procurement, pricing, inventory, and risk.
